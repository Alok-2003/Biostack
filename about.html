Biostack

Company: With the goal of providing environmentally friendly fuel products made from agricultural and forestry waste, BIOSTACK was established in India in 2022. Since then, the business has begun operating in India as a supplier of biomass briquettes and pellets, supplying a range of industries including paper mills, cement plants, and power plants. Everything we do is motivated by our dedication to environmental responsibility and sustainability. In order to reduce trash and build a more sustainable future, we think it's critical to use agricultural and forestry waste products. This conviction is reflected in the eco-friendly fuel products we proudly provide to our consumers. At BIOSTACK, we use cutting-edge production techniques and quality control procedures to make sure that our products are reliable and up to the highest standards of quality. Our skilled team of experts is committed to giving our clients outstanding service and support. We are committed to offering the best goods and solutions to fulfill our clients' specific needs after thoroughly listening to them in order to comprehend their applications and specific wants. Our goods are utilized for many different purposes, including heating, cooking, animal feed, and fertilizer.
