Briquettes

  • Briquettes are usually 1-2 kilos
  • Briquettes have a diameter of 70-350mm.
  • Calorific value range: 3,200 - 4,000 Kcal/Kg
  • Briquettes are primarily for industrial use for heating.

Pellets

  • pellets are bullet-sized.
  • Pellets have a diameter of 8-50mm.
  • Calorific value range: 3,200 - 4,100 Kcal/Kg
  • Pellets are preferred for household heating and some biomass power plants.
